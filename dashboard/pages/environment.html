About This Dataset

This dataset tracks the sustainability efforts and environmental impact during the Palkhi 2025 procession. It includes metrics like plastic waste collected, number of eco-volunteers involved, awareness campaigns held, and green initiatives per village. These insights help measure progress toward a cleaner and more responsible pilgrimage experience.
