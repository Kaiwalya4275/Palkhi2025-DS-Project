About This Dataset

This dataset showcases the various cultural and spiritual programs held during the Palkhi 2025 procession. It includes event names, timings, locations, organizing groups, expected audiences, and classification (music, dance, religious discourse, etc.). This data highlights the vibrant devotional culture and allows organizers to schedule and promote events more effectively.
