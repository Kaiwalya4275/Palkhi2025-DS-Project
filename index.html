About This Project

Palkhi 2025 is not just a dashboard it's a deeply researched, data-driven tribute to one of Maharashtra's most sacred traditions: the annual Sant Tukaram Maharaj Palkhi Sohala. This project captures the essence of this centuries-old spiritual journey using the power of modern data science and visual storytelling.

Through 10 uniquely designed datasets covering Dindi participation, transport logistics, food & water distribution, volunteers, cultural activities, medical support, security, route mapping, donations, and environmental impact, this dashboard transforms complex data into meaningful insights. Each dataset is rooted in realism, with authentic village names, structured leadership details, and dynamic patterns extracted using feature engineering, rule-based logic, and visual exploration.

The entire system is structured for scalability, interactivity, and aesthetic appeal. From animated dashboards to festival-themed visuals, the aim is to blend tradition with technology helping researchers, policymakers, and devotees alike understand the magnitude and elegance of the Palkhi Yatra through a modern lens.

This is not just a data project. It's a digital manifestation of faith, culture, and meticulous execution.
